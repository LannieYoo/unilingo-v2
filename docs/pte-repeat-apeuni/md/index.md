#### PTE Reading Guide
Multiple Choice Single Answer exam tipsThe Reading Multiple Choice Single Answer (MCS) question type accounts for only a very small proportion of marks in the overall Reading score. The most important technique for MCS is time management. Please complete each question of this type within 1 or 2 minutes. Otherwise, you will not have enough time for the more important FIB questions.[View more](https://www.apeuni.com/blog/r_mcs_guide_en?locale=en)Multiple Choice Multiple Answers exam tipsRe-order Paragraphs exam tipsFill in the Blanks (Drag and Drop) exam tipsFill in the Blanks (Dropdown) exam tips
