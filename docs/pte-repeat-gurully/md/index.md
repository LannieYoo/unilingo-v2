 How Is the CELPIP Score Calculated? 

CELPIP scores are based on your performance across the Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking sections. Each skill is evaluated separately and assigned a score according to the CELPIP proficiency scale.
 How Long Does CELPIP Result Take? 

CELPIP test results are typically available within 3–4 business days for the standard test. However, result timelines may vary depending on the test type and testing circumstances.
 How Can I Improve Fluency in PTE Speaking? 

Improving fluency in PTE Speaking requires regular practice, clear pronunciation, and confident delivery. Focus on speaking naturally without long pauses, expand your vocabulary, and take timed mock tests to become comfortable with the exam format and speaking tasks.
 PTE or IELTS: Which Test Is a Better Choice for Canada? 

Both PTE and IELTS are accepted for Canadian immigration and study purposes. The better choice depends on your strengths and preferences. PTE offers computer-based testing with faster results, while IELTS includes a face-to-face speaking test. Choose the exam format that best matches your comfort level and test-taking style.
 What Score in PTE Is Equivalent to 8 Bands in IELTS? 

A PTE Academic score of 79–82 is generally considered equivalent to an IELTS band score of 8.0. However, score conversions may vary slightly depending on the institution or immigration authority reviewing your results.
 For How Many Years Is the IELTS Result Valid? 

An IELTS score is valid for two years from the date of your test result. Most universities, colleges, employers, and immigration authorities accept IELTS scores within this validity period. If your score expires, you will generally need to retake the test to provide an updated proof of English proficiency.
