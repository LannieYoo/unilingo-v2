## What is the PTE?
The Pearson Test of English (PTE) is a computer-based exam that checks your English skills for study, work, or immigration. It tests [speaking](https://alfapte.com/pte-speaking-practice), [writing](https://alfapte.com/pte-writing-practice), [reading](https://alfapte.com/pte-reading-practice), and [listening](https://alfapte.com/pte-listening-practice) through real-life tasks. PTE scores are accepted by many universities, colleges, and governments around the world. Because the test is involves AI scoring and human expertise to reduce the risk of biases results are fast and consistent. ## Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about the PTE exam

### Who can take the PTE exam?
Anyone who wants to study, work, or migrate to a country that accepts PTE, and meets the age rules (usually 16+). Some test takers under 18 need parental consent.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/criteria-and-eligibility-for-pte-exam)

### What is the PTE exam duration?
PTE Academic, PTE Core, and PTE Academic UKVI all take around 2 hours.

### What is the format of the PTE Academic/Core/UKVI exam?
All three sections are computer-based and include:• Speaking & Writing (combined section)• Reading• ListeningYou complete all sections in one sitting, in about 2 hours.

### How is PTE different from other English language tests?
PTE is fully computer-based and AI scored with human evaluation, so results are fast and consistent. Test tasks use real-life academic and work situations. Many students find the format clear and the booking process flexible.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/differences-between-ielts-and-pte-exams)

### What is the difference between PTE Academic and PTE Core?
PTE Academic is used mainly for study and work visas in the majority of countries, and Permanent Residency visa applications in Australia and New Zealand.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/pte-core-vs-pte-academic-key-differences-for-study-work-pr-visas)

### What is the difference between PTE Academic and PTE Academic UKVI?
PTE Academic is used for studying in many countries, while PTE Academic UKVI is a special version approved by the UK government for certain UK study and work visas. The test content is the same, but the registration and rules follow UKVI standards.

### How can I prepare for the PTE exam?
The most effective approach is consistent practice with real exam-style questions, understanding scoring patterns, and taking AI-scored mock tests. AlfaPTE provides everything you need in one place.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/alfapte-best-choice-for-pte-preparation)

### How do I book the PTE exam?
Create an account on the official PTE website, choose your test (Academic, Core, or UKVI), pick a date and test center, then pay the fee online.You can visit [pearsonpte.com](https://www.pearsonpte.com) to book the exam[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/pte-booking)

### How can I find PTE exam dates?
Check live dates and available time slots on the official PTE website - [pearsonpte.com](https://www.pearsonpte.com)

### Where can I find a PTE test center?
Use the test center search tool on the official PTE website - [pearsonpte.com](https://www.pearsonpte.com) during booking.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/pte-acceptance)

### How much does the PTE exam cost?
Fees vary by country and test type. You can see the exact price for your location on the official PTE booking page - [pearsonpte.com](https://www.pearsonpte.com)[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/pte-acceptance)

### Can I retake the PTE test?
You can retake the PTE exam up to 12 times within a given 12-month period, but you must wait to receive your previous score before booking your next exam, and you can only book one test at a time. There's no overall lifetime limit, but you need to apply for an exception for more than 12 attempts, which is rarely granted.[Find Out More](https://alfapte.com/read-post/can-you-retake-pte-test)

### How long does it take to get PTE results?
PTE results officially take up to 5 business days, but most students receive them within 12–24 hours. You can view your scores directly in your PTE account.

### Who recognizes the PTE exam?
PTE is accepted by thousands of universities and colleges, and by governments for visas and migration (including Canada, the UK, Australia, and more).[Learn more](https://alfapte.com/pte-acceptance)
