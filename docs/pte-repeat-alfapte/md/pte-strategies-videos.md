## How our PTE Strategy Videos can help you achieve a higher score in other modules of PTE exam?
You can watch our PTE strategies videos to learn about some more interesting and efficient efficient PTE strategies which will teach you how you can score higher in modules in the actual PTE test.Some of the general tips included are:
![Stg First](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-strategies/stg_first.svg)Re-check Your Grammar, Spellings and VocabularyA mistake in vocabulary or grammar can change the meaning of a whole sentence or even make a sentence completely meaningless. Pay attention to the spellings too. Spelling mistakes in an English language skill test is unacceptable.
![Stg Second](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-strategies/stg_second.svg)Review Your AnswersBefore you submit, keep a little time to go through all the questions and answers. A review can save you a lot of mistakes at times.

![Stg Three](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-strategies/stg_three.svg)Apply Your Knowledge and ExperiencesWhile you take the test, for example a multiple-choice question, learn to predict the right answer without spending hours searching. Apply the experience you gain through practice and click on the most probable answer.
![Stg Fourth](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-strategies/stg_fourth.svg)Practice Reading and Speaking AloudReading aloud and speaking in a clear voice is important. Learn to pronounce words, vowels and consonants correctly. Check on the tone and texture of your voice while you speak. Don't repeat sentences. Practice reconstructing sentences if you need to.
