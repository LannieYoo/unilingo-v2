### What is the Read Aloud task in PTE Academic?
You will need to read a text displayed on the screen aloud. It tests your ability to pronounce words clearly, speak fluently, and use correct intonation while conveying the written content naturally.

### How much time do I have to prepare and read?
You will have 35-40 seconds to prepare after the text appears. Then, you will need to read the text aloud clearly and naturally.

### How is the Read Aloud task scored?
The task is scored automatically using AI-based evaluation, focusing on pronunciation, fluency, and oral reading accuracy. Correctly reading the entire text with clear articulation earns higher scores.

### How can I practice Read Aloud effectively?
You will need to practice regularly to improve your Read Aloud skills. Alfa PTE provides a smart platform for this, offering mock tests, sectional mock tests, and practice questions with AI scoring and instant feedback. Practicing on Alfa PTE helps you track your progress, identify mistakes, and perform confidently in the real exam.

### What are common mistakes to avoid in Read Aloud?
You will need to avoid speaking too fast or too slow, mispronouncing words, skipping text, or adding extra words. Speak naturally and follow the text exactly as written.

### What is the Respond to a Situation task?
You will need to read and listen to a description of a situation and respond appropriately. This task tests speaking, problem-solving, and communication skills.

### How much time do I have to think and answer?
You will have 10 seconds to prepare. After the beep, you will have 40 seconds to respond clearly and completely.

### How is this task scored?
AI scoring evaluates content relevance, clarity, fluency, and pronunciation. Giving a complete, logical response earns higher scores.

### How can I practice Respond to a Situation effectively?
You will need to practice using Alfa PTE mock tests, practice questions, and AI scoring, focusing on covering all aspects of the situation and responding naturally.

### What are common mistakes to avoid?
You will need to avoid giving incomplete answers, being off-topic, or speaking too quickly. Focus on clarity, completeness, and natural speech.
