### PTE Academic UKVI – Frequently Asked Questions
Get answers to all the frequently asked questions related to the PTE Academic UKVI exam variant.
![What is the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_1.svg)

### What is the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?
The PTE Academic UKVI is a computer-based English proficiency test designed to assess real-life English skills for work, migration, or study visa applications for the UK. It focuses on everyday communication.
![How is the PTE Academic UKVI different from PTE Core?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_2.svg)

### How is the PTE Academic UKVI different from PTE Core?
The PTE Academic UKVI has 22 tasks, whereas the PTE Core has 19 tasks. The PTE Academic UKVI has an essay writing task, summarizing group discussion, responding to a situation, and retelling lecture tasks, whereas the PTE Core has an email writing task.
![What are the modules in the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_3.svg)

### What are the modules in the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?
There are 4 modules, divided into 3 parts: Speaking and Writing, Reading, and Listening.
![What is the total duration of the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_4.svg)

### What is the total duration of the PTE Academic UKVI Exam?
The maximum time allotted for this test is 2 hours and 15 minutes.
![How is the PTE Academic UKVI Exam scored?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_10.svg)

### How is the PTE Academic UKVI Exam scored?
It follows the numerical score range of 10-90, which is evaluated by humans and AI. Humans only check the content part of a few of the questions, which still accounts to 14% of the total scoring process, and 86% is still evaluated by AI.
![How soon can I get the PTE Academic UKVI results?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_7.svg)

### How soon can I get the PTE Academic UKVI results?
Usually within 24-48 hours. As per the official source, it is a maximum of 5 working days excluding weekends.
![What is the exam format of the PTE Academic UKVI?](https://cdn-alfa-portal-react-js.alfapte.com/assets/svgs/pte-core-exam-faq-section/faq_2.svg)

### What is the exam format of the PTE Academic UKVI?
Firstly, it will begin with 7 tasks of speaking and 2 tasks of writing as a single part, which lasts about 76-84 minutes. Then, the next part of reading consists of 5 tasks, which takes about 23-30 minutes. Finally, the last part of the test is the Listening section, which has about 8 tasks, and is for 29-36 minutes.

There are 190+ test centres that can be used for the booking of the PTE Academic UKVI Exam.
