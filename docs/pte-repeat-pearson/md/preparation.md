## PTE Core Preparation

[See all FAQs](https://www.pearsonpte.com/help-center/preparation/)

[How can I prepare for PTE Core?](#)

You can prepare for PTE Core by practicing test questions, becoming familiar with the test format, and improving your English skills. Using official preparation materials and mock tests can help you understand how the test and scoring work, and build your confidence.

Common preparation methods include:

- Getting familiar and comfortable with the [test format](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-core/test-format/)

- Using official PTE preparation materials, such as our [Scored Practice Test](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-core/preparation/scored-practice-test/)

- Improving your speaking, writing, reading, and listening skills

[Can I use PTE Academic preparation materials to practice for PTE Core?](#)

No, the preparation materials for PTE Academic/PTE Academic UKVI are based on academic English content, whereas PTE Core is based on everyday English content.
