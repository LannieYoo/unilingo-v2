## What is PTE Academic?

PTE Academic is a computer-based academic English test for study, migration or work abroad. It is a 4-skills test – meaning it assesses your speaking, writing, reading and listening skills.

## What can you use PTE Academic for?

![](https://www.pearsonpte.com/content/dam/ELL/pte/pearsonpte/images/group-students-library-1000x500.jpg)

### For study worldwide

Choose PTE Academic if you plan to study at a university, college or trade school globally.

Accepted by virtually all universities in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, and the UK, and widely across the US – including all Ivy League universities.

![](https://www.pearsonpte.com/content/dam/ELL/pte/pearsonpte/images-optimized/australia-sydney-600x300.jpg)

### For migration to Australia or New Zealand

Choose PTE Academic if you plan to live in Australia or New Zealand.

Accepted by the Department of Home Affairs (DHA) in Australia and Immigration New Zealand for all immigration visa types for which an English test is required.

![](https://www.pearsonpte.com/content/dam/ELL/pte/pearsonpte/images/woman-standing-talking-to-people-at-work-1000x500.jpg)

### For work

Choose PTE Academic if you require a work visa for Australia or New Zealand, or if you are registering with a professional association in the USA.

Accepted by the Department of Home Affairs (DHA), Immigration New Zealand, and by the US state Boards of Nursing and Health Resources & Services Administration (HRSA).

## Frequently asked questions

[See all PTE Academic FAQs](https://www.pearsonpte.com/help-center/general-faqs/pte-academic/)

Who accepts PTE Academic?

PTE Academic is accepted by:

- 4,000+ universities and colleges worldwide, including virtually all universities in Australia, Canada, UK and New Zealand, and widely across the US
- The Australia and New Zealand governments for migration visas
- The Health Resources & Services Administration (HRSA) and the US state Boards of Nursing for nursing professional registration

[Find institutions that accept PTE Academic](https://www.pearsonpte.com/who-accepts-pte/)

How much does PTE Academic cost?

The fees for PTE Academic vary depending on the country in which you choose to take your test. To find your price, use the **price finder tool at the top of this page**, or on our [test centers and fees page](https://www.pearsonpte.com/test-centers-and-fees/).

How do I receive my PTE Academic score?

After you have taken your test, you will be notified by email when your PTE Academic scores are released and are ready to view.

You can check your PTE Academic scores by logging into your myPTE account:

1. Log in to your [myPTE](#sign-in-modal) account
2. Go to My Activity
3. Select your test to view your Score Report

Your Score Report will include:

- Your overall score
- Individual scores for speaking, writing, reading, and listening

How long is a PTE Academic score valid for?

**A PTE Academic score is valid for two years from the test date**. After this period, the score expires and can no longer be used for study or visa applications.

If your score expires, you will need to take the PTE Academic test again to obtain a new valid score.

Most education institutions and immigration authorities require a valid English test taken within the last two years.

What is the difference between PTE Academic and PTE Academic UKVI?

PTE Academic and [PTE Academic UKVI](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-academic-ukvi/) have the same test content, format, and scoring. The only difference is that the UKVI test will provide you with a Unique Reference Number (URN) that you use for some UK visa applications.

**Choose PTE Academic for: **

- Study applications to UK universities / Higher Education Institutions

**Choose PTE Academic UKVI for: **

- UK work visas that require a 4-skills test (Skilled Worker, Startup, Innovator, and Minister of Religion visas)
- UK visas for study pathways ***below*** degree level, or for places that are ***not*** a Higher Education Institution

It is important to check the requirements listed by your university or the UK Home Office before booking your test.

Where do I take PTE Academic? Can I take it at home?

Like all PTE tests, PTE Academic is taken at an authorized PTE test center, on a computer. This ensures that every test is secure, reliable, and fair – for you and and for the universities, governments, and other institutions that accept PTE. PTE Academic cannot be taken at home.

[Find your nearest PTE test center](https://mypte.pearsonpte.com/test/search?examCode=PTE-A)

How long does it take to get PTE Academic results?

Most test takers get their PTE Academic results within two business days after taking the test. In some cases, results may take up to five business days, depending on processing times.

You will be notified by email when your Score Report is ready.

**To view your results: **

- Log in to your [myPTE account](https://mypte.pearsonpte.com/)
- Go to My Activity
- Select your test to see your score report

Can I reschedule or cancel my PTE Academic exam?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your PTE test through your myPTE account.

**Rescheduling your exam**: you can reschedule for free up to 14 days before your test date. If you need to reschedule within 14 days of your test, you need to cancel your appointment and re-book your test. Fees will apply.

**Fees for canceling your exam**:

- ***14 or more days before the test***: cancel for free
- ***Between 13 and 8 days***: you will receive a 50% refund
- ***Fewer than 7 days***: you do not receive a refund. PTE may make exeptions in exceptional circumstances, at our discretion.

[Learn more about our cancellation and rescheduling policy](https://www.pearsonpte.com/policy-center/cancellations-rescheduling/)

**To reschedule or cancel your booking: **

- Log in to your [myPTE](https://mypte.pearsonpte.com/) account
- Go to My Activity
- Select your booking and choose Reschedule or Cancel
