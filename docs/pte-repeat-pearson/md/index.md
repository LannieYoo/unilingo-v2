## Frequently asked questions

[See all PTE Core FAQs](https://www.pearsonpte.com/help-center/general-faqs/pte-core/)

What is the difference between PTE Academic and PTE Core?

[PTE Academic](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-academic/) and PTE Core are designed for different visa pathways:

- **PTE Academic** is used for university admissions (including in Canada), and migration visas to Australia and New Zealand.
- **PTE Core** is used just for Canadian permanent residency and citizenship visas.

In essence, if you want to apply for a migration visa in Canada, choose PTE Core. If you wish to study in Canada, choose PTE Academic.

**The test content and tasks are different, too**: PTE Core is a general English test that assesses language that might be used everyday in a workplace or community; PTE Academic is an academic English test and the questions reflect language you might hear in an academic setting.

Both tests are 4-skills tests (assessing speaking, writing, reading, and listening skills), are taken on a computer at a test center, and use similar scoring methods.

How much does PTE Core cost?

The fees for PTE Core vary depending on the country in which you choose to take your test. To find your price, use the **price finder tool at the top of this page**, or on our [test centers and fees page](https://www.pearsonpte.com/test-centers-and-fees/).

How long is a PTE Core score valid for?

**A PTE Core score is valid for two years from the test date**. After this period, the score expires and can no longer be used for visa applications.

If your score expires, you will need to take the PTE Core test again to obtain a new valid score.

How long is the PTE Core test?

PTE Core takes **around two hours** to complete. The test is computer-based and split into three sections:

- Speaking & Writing
- Reading
- Listening

[Learn more about the PTE Core test format](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-core/test-format/)

How long does it take to get my PTE Core results?

Most test takers get their PTE Core results within two business days after taking the test. In some cases, results may take up to five business days, depending on processing times.

You will be notified by email when your Score Report is ready.

**To view your results: **

- Log in to your [myPTE account](https://mypte.pearsonpte.com/)
- Go to My Activity
- Select your test to see your score report

How do I access my PTE Core score?

After you have taken your test, you will be notified by email when your PTE Core scores are released and are ready to view.

You can check your PTE Core scores by logging into your myPTE account:

1. Log in to your [myPTE](#sign-in-modal) account
2. Go to My Activity
3. Select your test to view your Score Report

Your Score Report will include:

- Your overall score
- Individual scores for speaking, writing, reading, and listening

Can I reschedule or cancel my PTE Core exam?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your PTE test through your myPTE account.

**Rescheduling your exam**: you can reschedule for free up to 14 days before your test date. If you need to reschedule within 14 days of your test, you need to cancel your appointment and re-book your test. Fees will apply.

**Fees for canceling your exam**:

- ***14 or more days before the test***: cancel for free
- ***Between 13 and 8 days***: you will receive a 50% refund
- ***Fewer than 7 days***: you do not receive a refund. PTE may make exeptions in exceptional circumstances, at our discretion.

[Learn more about our cancellation and rescheduling policy](https://www.pearsonpte.com/policy-center/cancellations-rescheduling/)

**To reschedule or cancel your booking: **

- Log in to your [myPTE](https://mypte.pearsonpte.com/) account
- Go to My Activity
- Select your booking and choose Reschedule or Cancel

Can I take PTE Core at home?

Like all PTE tests, PTE Core is taken at an authorized PTE test center, on a computer. This ensures that every test is secure, reliable, and fair – for you and and for any governments, university, or institution that accepts PTE.

PTE Core cannot be taken at home.
