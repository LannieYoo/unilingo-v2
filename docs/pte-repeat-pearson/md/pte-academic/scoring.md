## How is PTE Academic scored?

Our scoring is designed to give you the fairest possible result.

You won't interact with a human examiner, reducing the risk of human bias. Instead, your test will be marked by our sophisticated AI scoring system — trained by experts, and refined daily by millions of data points.

Multiple choice answers are scored as right or wrong, but scores for more complex question types, like essay questions, are based on a mix of criteria. Some responses are also reviewed by a human expert before the automated score is finalised — helping to ensure scores are fair and accurate

## Your personalized Score Report and Skills Profile

When you complete your test, you will receive your Score Report and Skills Profile, which help you understand more about your test score, language skill level and where you can improve.

### What you will see on your Score Report

You will receive an overall score of between 10 and 90, with 90 being the highest possible score. You will also get individual scores for your communicative skills to identify your strengths and areas for improvement. Scores are graded against the Global Scale of English (GSE).

### What you will see on your Skills Profile

Your Skills Profile gives you a detailed report of your performance across different language skills, with personal recommendations on how to improve your English proficiency even further.
