Whether PTE is easier than IELTS depends on the individual's strengths. PTE is computer-based with automated scoring, which some test-takers find more straightforward. IELTS includes human evaluators, which may suit those who prefer face-to-face interaction in the speaking section.

The choice between IELTS and PTE depends on your personal preference and the specific requirements of the institution or country you are applying to. Both tests are widely recognized, but PTE is entirely computer-based, while IELTS offers both computer-based and paper-based options.
